Our Story

Building Tomorrow's
Leaders

Pioneer Public College Batkhela stands as a beacon of quality education in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. Since our founding, our institution has been built on the foundation of academic rigour, moral values, and student-centred learning.

With experienced faculty, modern facilities, and an unwavering commitment to producing outstanding results, Pioneer Public College has consistently ranked among the top institutions in the region — a name synonymous with trust and excellence.

  • Highly qualified and dedicated teaching faculty
  • Modern, fully-equipped science and computer labs
  • Comprehensive library and academic resources
  • Regular parent-teacher communication & progress reports
  • Merit-based and need-based scholarship programmes

Admission Process

Simple, transparent, and merit-based — begin your journey with Pioneer Public College in four straightforward steps.

01

Download Form

Download the admission form, print it, and fill in your personal and academic details carefully.

02

Submit Documents

Submit your matric result card, CNIC/B-Form, domicile certificate, and two passport-size photographs at the college office.

03

Appear in Entry Test

Sit our entry assessment to gauge your academic readiness and potential for your chosen programme.

04

Complete Enrollment

Finalise fee payment and receive your official student ID to begin your academic journey with us.
